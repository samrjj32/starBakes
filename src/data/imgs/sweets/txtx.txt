1.Peda
Pedas is a sweet that is usually made in semi- soft thick pieces.


2.Kaju Katli
Kaju katli is an Indian sweet dish. Kaju in Hindi is known as cashew nut.


3.Ladoo
Laddu are ball-shaped sweets popular in the. Laddus are prepared with flour, kneaded dough and sugar.

4.Imarti
Imarti is a dessert introduced by Mughal cooks.


5.Jalebi
Jalebi is made by deep-frying the maida flour batter which is mostly in circular shapes, which is then dipped in sugar syrup to add more sweetness.
